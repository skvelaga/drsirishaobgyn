About Dr. Sirisha

A Personal Approach to Expert Women's Medicine

Dr. Velaga Sirisha is a highly accomplished Obstetrician, Gynecologist, Laparoscopic Surgeon, and Urogynecologist serving as Senior Consultant at Arete Hospitals, West Hyderabad.

With over 9 years of clinical experience, she believes that modern healthcare should be as precise as it is personal — listening first, understanding the narrative behind every patient's journey before crafting a truly tailored plan of care.
