About

Committed to Women's Health

Dr. Velaga Sirisha is a highly accomplished Obstetrician, Gynecologist, Laparoscopic Surgeon, and Urogynecologist. She serves as Senior Consultant at Arete Hospitals, West Hyderabad. With over 9 years of clinical experience, a DNB from the National Board of Examinations, and dedicated fellowships in both Advanced Laparoscopic Surgery and Urogynecology, she brings specialized surgical expertise to women across Hyderabad.

A textbook contributor, Dr. Sirisha is renowned for her compassionate approach and her ability to explain complex medical conditions in simple terms — in Telugu, Hindi, English, or Tamil. She specializes in minimally invasive surgery, ensuring minimal pain and quicker recovery for her patients.

Speaks: 🗣️ Telugu 🗣️ Hindi 🗣️ English 🗣️ Tamil
